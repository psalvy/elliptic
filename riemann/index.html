Riemann Zeta Function & the Primes

An interactive course tracing the road from Euler's Basel sum to the Riemann Hypothesis. The central payoff is the explicit formula — a contour integral that proves primes and zeros of $\zeta$ are two sides of the same coin.

    Prerequisites: comfort with complex numbers, basic calculus (series and integrals), and the idea of a pole. Familiarity with the Elliptic Curves course is helpful but not required.
