Elliptic Curves & Ramanujan's Approximations of π

An interactive course connecting the geometry of tori, the algebra of elliptic curves, and Ramanujan's extraordinary formulas for π. Each module builds on the last — tap to begin.

  • Module 1
    Demystifying Quotient Spaces
    Visualize $\mathbb{C}/\Lambda$: from lattice to torus, interactively.
  • Module 2
    Elliptic Curves over ℝ
    Weierstrass form $y^2 = x^3 + ax + b$, point addition, and the group law.
  • Module 3
    Elliptic Curves over ℂ
    The Weierstrass ℘-function: how a flat torus becomes an elliptic curve.
  • Module 4
    The j-invariant & Modular Forms
    Fingerprinting curves — the bridge from geometry to number theory.
  • Interlude
    The Grand Unification
    Drag $\tau$ in the upper half-plane — watch the lattice, torus, and algebraic curve morph live.
  • Module 5
    Complex Multiplication & Ramanujan's π
    How special lattice symmetries unlock mind-bending π formulas.
Prerequisites: comfort with complex numbers, Euler's formula ($e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta$), and the idea of a Riemann surface. Everything else is built up visually.
