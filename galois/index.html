Galois Theory & Elliptic Curves

From permuting roots of a quadratic to the Galois representations that explain why complex multiplication forces algebraic values. This course closes the loop on the Elliptic Curves course — the algebra behind why the arguments there actually work.

    Prerequisites: the Elliptic Curves course (Modules 1–7), comfort with polynomials and complex numbers. Groups and fields are built up from scratch — no prior abstract algebra assumed.
